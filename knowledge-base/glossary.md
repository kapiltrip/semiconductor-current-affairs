# Semiconductor Glossary

## ATMP

Assembly, testing, marking, and packaging. Often used in India policy discussions for post-fab semiconductor manufacturing.

## CoWoS

Chip-on-Wafer-on-Substrate. TSMC's advanced packaging platform widely used for AI accelerators that need high-bandwidth memory close to compute dies.

## DFT

Design for test. VLSI methodology for making chips testable after manufacturing.

## DLI

Design Linked Incentive. Indian government support scheme for semiconductor design companies.

## EDA

Electronic design automation. Software tools used for chip design, verification, synthesis, place and route, timing analysis, physical verification, and signoff.

## EUV

Extreme ultraviolet lithography. Used for manufacturing the most advanced semiconductor nodes.

## Fab

A semiconductor fabrication plant where wafers are processed into chips.

## Fabless

A company that designs chips but outsources manufacturing to foundries.

## Foundry

A company that manufactures chips designed by other companies.

## GAA

Gate-all-around transistor. A transistor architecture used for advanced nodes after FinFET scaling becomes harder.

## HBM

High bandwidth memory. Stacked DRAM used in AI accelerators and high-performance computing.

## IDM

Integrated device manufacturer. A company that designs and manufactures its own chips, such as Intel historically and Samsung in parts of its business.

## Node

Marketing and technology label for a semiconductor manufacturing generation, such as 5nm, 3nm, or 2nm. The number no longer directly equals a physical transistor dimension.

## OSAT

Outsourced semiconductor assembly and test. Companies that package and test chips after wafer fabrication.

## RTL

Register-transfer level. A hardware design abstraction commonly written in Verilog, SystemVerilog, or VHDL.

## Tapeout

The final design handoff to manufacturing.

## WFE

Wafer fabrication equipment. Tools used in wafer manufacturing, including lithography, etch, deposition, metrology, inspection, cleaning, and CMP.
