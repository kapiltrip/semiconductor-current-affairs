# Semiconductor Industry Map

Use this page to understand where each news item fits in the value chain.

## 1. Design

Companies design chips but may not manufacture them.

Examples:

- NVIDIA: GPUs, AI accelerators, networking
- AMD: CPUs, GPUs, adaptive SoCs
- Qualcomm: mobile SoCs, modems, automotive
- Apple: custom SoCs
- Broadcom: networking, custom ASICs
- MediaTek: mobile and connectivity SoCs

What to track:

- Architecture changes
- AI accelerator roadmaps
- HBM usage
- Custom silicon wins
- IP blocks and chiplet strategy

## 2. EDA And IP

EDA tools and IP are required to design and verify chips.

Examples:

- Synopsys
- Cadence
- Siemens EDA
- Arm

What to track:

- Design automation for AI chips
- Verification complexity
- 3D-IC and chiplet design flows
- IP licensing and Arm ecosystem changes

## 3. Foundry And Manufacturing

Foundries manufacture chips for fabless companies.

Examples:

- TSMC
- Samsung Foundry
- Intel Foundry
- GlobalFoundries
- UMC
- SMIC

What to track:

- Advanced nodes: N3, N2, 18A, 14A, GAA
- Capacity expansion
- Yield
- Customer wins
- Advanced packaging integration

## 4. Memory

Memory is critical for AI, mobile, PCs, servers, and automotive.

Examples:

- SK hynix
- Samsung Memory
- Micron

What to track:

- HBM supply and pricing
- DRAM cycle
- NAND cycle
- Advanced DRAM scaling
- Customer qualification for AI accelerators

## 5. Equipment

Equipment suppliers make the machines used in fabs.

Examples:

- ASML: lithography
- Applied Materials: deposition, materials engineering
- Lam Research: etch and deposition
- KLA: process control, inspection, metrology
- Tokyo Electron: deposition, etch, cleaning, coating/developing

What to track:

- EUV and High-NA EUV demand
- WFE spending
- China export restrictions
- Regional fab buildout

## 6. Materials And Substrates

Materials are required across wafer manufacturing and packaging.

Examples:

- Silicon wafers
- Photoresists
- Specialty gases
- Chemicals
- ABF substrates
- Advanced packaging substrates

What to track:

- Supply shortages
- Japan/Korea/Taiwan materials ecosystem
- Export controls
- HBM and advanced packaging material demand

## 7. Packaging, Assembly, And Test

Packaging connects chips to the system and is now a major performance constraint.

Examples:

- ASE
- Amkor
- JCET
- Tata Electronics
- Kaynes
- CG Power

What to track:

- OSAT / ATMP expansion
- CoWoS, fan-out, 2.5D, 3D stacking
- Chiplets
- Thermal and power delivery challenges
- India packaging projects

## 8. Policy And Geopolitics

Semiconductors are now strategic infrastructure.

What to track:

- US export controls
- China self-sufficiency
- Taiwan risk
- CHIPS Act
- EU Chips Act
- Japan and Korea incentives
- India Semiconductor Mission

## Mental Model

When reading news, classify it like this:

```text
News item -> Value-chain segment -> Companies/countries -> Technical concept -> Business impact -> Interview question
```
