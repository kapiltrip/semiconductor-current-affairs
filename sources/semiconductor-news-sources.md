# Semiconductor News Sources

Use primary sources first, then trade publications and analyst summaries. Prefer original press releases, investor pages, regulatory notices, and official industry bodies when numbers matter.

## Tier 1: Primary Sources

| Source | Use It For | Cadence | Link |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Semiconductor Industry Association (SIA) | Monthly global semiconductor sales, policy positions, US industry investment | Monthly | https://www.semiconductors.org/news-events/latest-news/ |
| World Semiconductor Trade Statistics (WSTS) | Global semiconductor market forecasts by product and region | Quarterly / semiannual | https://www.wsts.org/ |
| SEMI Market Intelligence | Fab spending, equipment billings, capacity, materials, advanced packaging | Monthly / quarterly | https://www.semi.org/en/taxonomy/term/45726 |
| TSMC Investor Relations | Monthly revenue, quarterly earnings, node and capacity commentary | Monthly / quarterly | https://investor.tsmc.com/english |
| ASML Investor Relations | EUV/DUV demand, backlog, lithography outlook, High-NA EUV commentary | Quarterly | https://www.asml.com/investors |
| Intel Investor Relations | Foundry, process roadmap, IDM 2.0, 18A/14A updates | Quarterly | https://www.intc.com/ |
| Samsung Newsroom / IR | Memory, foundry, GAA, HBM, capex, advanced packaging | Quarterly | https://semiconductor.samsung.com/newsroom/ |
| NVIDIA Investor Relations | AI accelerators, data center demand, HBM ecosystem, networking | Quarterly | https://investor.nvidia.com/ |
| Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) | US export controls on AI chips, semiconductor equipment, China restrictions | Event-driven | https://www.bis.gov/news-updates |
| India Semiconductor Mission (ISM) | India fabs, OSAT/ATMP, compound semiconductors, DLI, policy schemes | Event-driven | https://ism.gov.in/ |
| Press Information Bureau (PIB), India | Official Indian government semiconductor announcements | Event-driven | https://www.pib.gov.in/ |

## What To Capture From Each Source

- Date
- Source URL
- Company or country involved
- Segment: design, foundry, memory, equipment, EDA/IP, packaging, policy, market
- One-line summary
- Why it matters
- Technical concept to learn
- Confidence: primary, strong secondary, weak/unverified
